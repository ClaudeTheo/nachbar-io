The Problem

Europe is aging. The infrastructure isn’t ready.

5.6 million people in Germany need care. Most want to stay home. But the system is failing them.

⏱

Delayed Emergencies

Seniors living alone may not be found for hours or days after a fall or medical event.

💊

Missed Medication

Without reminders and tracking, medication adherence drops dramatically.

💔

Loneliness Epidemic

Social isolation is as deadly as smoking 15 cigarettes a day. Millions are affected.

💪

Caregiver Overload

Family and professional caregivers are stretched thin with no shared coordination tools.

🔌

Fragmented Systems

Neighbors, family, caregivers, and municipalities don’t share a common platform.

The Solution

Turn neighborhoods into care networks.

Nachbar.io connects seniors, neighbors, families, caregivers, and municipalities through one system — combining a modern web platform with dedicated hardware for people who can’t use smartphones.

The neighborhood is the smallest and most effective unit of care. Nachbar.io makes that network visible, organized, and reliable.
